// 01 — about

Who I am.

My name is Mason Good and I'm based in Tampa, Florida. I'm completing my degree in Information Systems at USF with a focus on data science. Since as early as I can remember I was engrossed in technology — helping my mom fix the cords on the VHS and DVD players, that kind of thing. That curiosity never went away.

It led me to video games, then to IT, and eventually down the data science path. I love knowledge and I love learning, and data science is all about information. That pull has only grown stronger as my career has progressed.

My journey started at Best Buy's Geek Squad, where I learned that great IT isn't just about fixing things — it's about understanding people's needs. That foundation led me through infrastructure management at a credit union, and now to Process Panda, where I build custom ITSM solutions for enterprise clients like Perdue Farms and the City of San Jose.

// where I'm headed

I know where I want to go — the move now is closing the gap between the IT work I do every day and the analytics depth the data science field demands. My IS coursework gave me a real foundation in k-NN, logistic regression, clustering, and SVM, but I'm pushing that further deliberately outside the classroom.

The certifications that make sense next are on the analytics side. I already sit across the table from organizations every day as a consultant. Data scientist is the fourth fastest growing occupation in the country right now — and I already understand how these organizations actually work.
